## Key Finding

Hedera offers **75-90% cost reduction** with equivalent or superior functionality vs Energy Web and Power Ledger.

## SWOT Summary

**Strengths**: 75-90% cost reduction, AI verification, emerging market focus, testnet evidence  
**Weaknesses**: No established market presence, Verra approval pending, newer technology  
**Opportunities**: 500,000+ small hydro plants globally, $2.5-5B addressable market  
**Threats**: Incumbent relationships (EW, PL), regulatory uncertainty  

## Market Opportunity

- Target: Small hydropower plants in emerging markets (100,000+ plants)
- Average annual RECs per plant: 100-500
- Average REC price: $10-20
- Potential annual revenue: $100M-$1B
